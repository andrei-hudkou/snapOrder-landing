## 6 Detailed Prompts for Launch Creatives

Use these in Canva Magic Media (or your image/video generator).  
Tip: if result quality is weak, run 3 generations and pick best 1.

### 1) `C1_DM_Chaos_Static` (Image, Pain)

**Primary prompt**
Create a realistic mobile ad image at exact size 1080x1350 (4:5). Subject: solo handmade seller at a small home workspace, looking stressed while checking Instagram DMs on phone. Composition: seller on right third of frame, phone visible in hand with many unread chat bubbles (generic, no real logos), messy sticky notes and packaging materials on desk. Lighting: soft natural daylight from window, slightly dramatic contrast. Color direction: neutral background, brand accent colors only #6941c6, #53389e, #1d2939 in UI highlights and objects. Leave clean negative space in top 20% for headline overlay and bottom 12% for CTA. Style: photorealistic, modern ecommerce creator vibe, not corporate stock photo. Do not render any words, logos, watermarks, or gibberish text.

**Fallback prompt**
Create a 1080x1350 realistic ad visual: close-up of phone with overflowing unread order messages and a worried seller in blurred background. Keep top area empty for text overlay. Brand accents #6941c6 #53389e #1d2939. No logos, no text in image.

### 2) `C2_Followup_Time_Static` (Image, Time)

**Primary prompt**
Create a realistic split-scene ad image at exact size 1080x1350 (4:5). Left side: seller manually copying order info from chat to notebook with tired expression, clock showing late evening. Right side: same seller calmer, viewing a clean order list UI on phone/tablet. Show clear before/after contrast without using text. Camera angle: eye-level, medium shot. Lighting: left side warmer and dimmer, right side cleaner and brighter. Keep center-top 18% and lower 12% visually simple for text overlay. Brand accents only #6941c6, #53389e, #1d2939 in UI and props. Style: practical small-business realism, no glossy startup look. No logos, no readable words, no watermark.

**Fallback prompt**
Generate 1080x1350 before-vs-after creative for DM order admin time waste. Left = notebook + chats + stress, right = clean order board + relief. Maintain strong visual contrast and empty top space for headline.

### 3) `C3_AllInOne_Control_Static` (Image, Control)

**Primary prompt**
Create a clean confidence-focused ad image at exact size 1080x1350 (4:5). Subject: solo micro-brand seller in workshop holding phone with a simple order dashboard mockup (cards for customer, item, status, due date). Seller expression: calm and in control. Background: shelves with handmade products and shipping boxes, softly blurred. Composition: phone and seller centered, with quiet negative space in top 15% and bottom 15% for copy and CTA. Visual style: modern realistic product photo, minimal clutter, high readability on mobile feed. Brand color accents: #6941c6 #53389e #1d2939 only. Avoid extra bright colors. No embedded text, no logos, no watermark.

**Fallback prompt**
Create 1080x1350 realistic ad image of seller confidently managing all DM orders from one clean mobile dashboard. Keep scene minimal and text-safe areas empty.

### 4) `C4_UGC_Pain_Video` (Video, Pain)

**Primary prompt**
Generate a vertical UGC-style video at exact size 1080x1920, 15 seconds, handheld smartphone look. Character: solo handmade seller talking directly to camera in home workshop. Shot plan: 0-2s hook close-up face, concerned expression; 2-6s cut to phone screen with chaotic unread order chats and sticky notes; 6-11s show simple upload screenshot flow and draft order card appearing; 11-15s return to speaker with relief and clear CTA moment. Tone: practical, honest, non-hype. Add burned-in captions with high contrast, large sans-serif, centered safely (avoid top 250px and bottom 250px). Keep brand accents subtle: #6941c6 #53389e #1d2939. No logos, no copyrighted UI, no glitch effects.

**Fallback prompt**
Create 1080x1920 15-second creator-style testimonial: problem (lost DM orders) -> simple workflow -> relief -> CTA. Handheld authentic footage, strong captions, no flashy transitions.

### 5) `C5_UGC_Time_Video` (Video, Time)

**Primary prompt**
Generate a vertical video at exact size 1080x1920, duration 15 seconds, realistic small-business UGC style. Story arc: 0-3s seller switching between Instagram/WhatsApp chat, notes app, spreadsheet; 3-8s repetitive typing and copy-paste with tired body language; 8-12s screenshot upload and structured order draft appears; 12-15s short CTA end card. Camera: quick cuts, medium and close-up shots, handheld feel. Captions required on every spoken line, large and readable. Keep first hook visible in first 1.5 seconds. Maintain text-safe zones (no critical text in top/bottom UI areas). Color accents: #6941c6 #53389e #1d2939. No logo overlays, no complex motion graphics.

**Fallback prompt**
Produce 1080x1920 15-second time-saving ad with clear before/after workflow and strong subtitle readability for mobile.

### 6) `C6_Motion_Control_Video` (Video, Control)

**Primary prompt**
Generate a vertical motion graphic video at exact size 1080x1920, duration 15 seconds, clean minimal style. Sequence: 0-3s incoming DM screenshot card animation; 3-7s AI extraction animation reveals fields (customer, item, quantity, deadline); 7-11s status chips animate through New -> Confirmed -> Packed; 11-15s calm end card with control message and CTA area. Visual language: simple UI cards, smooth 200-300ms transitions, no heavy effects. Color system only: #6941c6, #53389e, #1d2939, white, light gray backgrounds. Typography: bold sans-serif, high contrast, very legible on phone. Keep critical text within middle safe region; avoid top/bottom UI overlap zones. No logo clutter, no neon colors, no noisy backgrounds.

**Fallback prompt**
Create 1080x1920 15-second simple UI motion ad showing DM screenshot to tracked order pipeline with clear status progression and clean CTA end frame.
