## 4. Message-by-Channel Guide

### Landing Page
- Above fold: Variant A or B headline, single CTA, no navigation
- Section 2: "Screenshot → Upload → Order tracked" — use the word "seconds"
- Section 3: ICP mirror language — situations, not features ("I have 200 DMs and no system")
- Section 4: 4 outcome bullets — "Never forget a follow-up," "Know instantly who hasn't paid," "All your orders in one place," "Works the way you already work"
- Words to use: lose, forget, track, follow up, screenshot, order, paid, DM
- Words to avoid: CRM, pipeline, workflow, integration, automation, omnichannel

### Instagram/Facebook Ads
- Angle 1 (Awareness): Problem recognition — chaotic DM thread visual, "you're probably losing some"
- Angle 2 (Consideration): Mechanism reveal — before/after: DM screenshot → clean order card
- Angle 3 (Remarketing): Social proof mirror — "For Instagram sellers tired of losing orders"

### Cold Outreach (DM/Community)
- Never use "CRM" in the first message
- Template A (pain): "Do you manage your Instagram orders in notes, spreadsheets, or your head?"
- Template B (mechanism): "I'm building a tool where you screenshot a DM and it creates a structured order. Would that be useful for how you work?"

### Product Hunt / Communities (IH, Reddit, HN)
- Lead with the founder insight, not the product
- Language: "Instagram sellers already screenshot conversations — I'm just making that screenshot useful"
- Share validation data, not feature lists

## 5. Message Risks

| ID | Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|----|------|----------|------------|
| M-001 | "CRM" label creates category repulsion | High | Remove from all ICP-facing copy. Keep in SEO meta only. |
| M-002 | "AI" triggers skepticism before trial | Medium | Lead with outcome ("your order is ready"), not mechanism ("AI reads it") |
| M-003 | Screenshot behavior not validated — entire hook may break | High | Add survey on landing page: "How do you currently track DM orders?" |
| M-004 | Privacy/data trust concern from sellers uploading customer DMs | Medium-High | Add trust copy near CTA: "Screenshots processed and deleted. We never store customer messages." |
| M-005 | "Waitlist" framing reduces urgency | Low-Medium | Use "early access" framing. Add social proof count as soon as numbers are real. |
