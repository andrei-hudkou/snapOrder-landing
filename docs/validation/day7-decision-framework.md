## 2) Apply Final Decision Threshold

Retain a creative only if:

- creative spend reached at least `$5`, and
- `CTR > baseline median CTR`, and
- `CPL < baseline median CPL`

Kill a creative if:

- `CTR < 0.8%`, or
- `CPL > $6`, or
- no leads after around `$7` spend

## 3) Classify Sprint Outcome

### A) Continue and Expand

Use when at least one creative consistently beats baseline on CTR and CPL.

Next action:
- run a 14-day follow-up with focus on winning angle and format.

### B) Keep Ads, Improve Landing

Use when CTR is acceptable but CPL is weak.

Interpretation:
- ad hook works, landing conversion is weak.

Next action:
- simplify hero message and form copy, keep winning ad live.

### C) Rework Messaging

Use when CTR and leads are weak.

Interpretation:
- ad message does not resonate with ICP.

Next action:
- build a fresh 6-creative set with sharper pain/time/control hooks.
