# SnapOrder Solo Runbook (Beginner, 7 Days, $40)

This is the exact execution order. Follow it step by step.

## Inputs and Boundaries

- Channel: Meta Ads only
- Total budget: `$40`
- Launch creatives on Day 1: `6` max
- Mobile-first only
- Goal: learn fast, not polish

## Linked Docs

- Preflight: `docs/validation/preflight-setup-7day-40usd.md`
- Creative pack: `docs/validation/creative-batch-7day-40usd.md`
- Launch checklist: `docs/validation/meta-launch-checklist-7day-40usd.md`
- Daily tracker: `docs/validation/daily-optimization-tracker-7day-40usd.md`
- Day-7 decision: `docs/validation/day7-decision-framework.md`

## Day-by-Day Actions

## Day 0 - Setup

1. Complete all checks in preflight doc.
2. Confirm form endpoint works on mobile.
3. Copy the tracking template CSV into Google Sheets.
4. Confirm creative IDs are fixed as `C1` through `C6`.

## Day 1 - Produce + Launch

1. Create 3 static and 3 video creatives from prompt pack.
2. Export with exact required sizes.
3. Launch all 6 creatives in one campaign.
4. Do not change anything for first 24 hours unless broken.

## Day 2 - First Decisions

1. Update creative-level metrics in tracker.
2. Apply minimum spend gate: evaluate only if spend is `>= $5`.
3. Calculate baseline medians for CTR and CPL.
4. Mark each creative: keep, kill, or iterate.

## Day 3 - Reallocate

1. Move budget to top 2 creatives.
2. Keep one top performer unchanged as control.
3. Kill clear losers by threshold rules.

## Day 4 - Controlled Iteration

1. Create one variant from best performer.
2. Change one variable only (hook or first frame or headline).
3. Compare variant vs control after minimum spend.

## Day 5 - Format Decision

1. Compare static vs video winner by CPL and CTR.
2. Shift majority spend to winning format.
3. Keep one challenger live for validation.

## Day 6 - Final Pruning

1. Pause creatives that fail thresholds.
2. Keep only creatives beating baseline.
3. Prepare summary notes for Day 7.

## Day 7 - Decision and Handoff

1. Use Day-7 framework to classify result.
2. Record:
   - winning creative(s),
   - winning angle,
   - winning format,
   - baseline and final CPL.
3. Write next-week one-page action brief.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- Never launch more than 6 creatives on Day 1.
- Never change more than one variable in a test.
- Never judge a creative before minimum spend threshold.
- Keep at least 2 creatives active until Day 6.
